AstaChemma Rules

Pieces

Each player has 3 Knights and 1 King.

Type-locked Combat

Knights eliminate Knights only; Kings eliminate Kings only. Wrong match = illegal move.

Safe Zones

No elimination in Safe Zones. Stacking is allowed only in Base and Safe Zones.

King Move Rule

King moves only on even dice totals; odd totals become illegal for the King.

Inner Zone Lock

Inner zone opens only after you make 1 elimination (with any piece).

Core Entry (25)

Exact entry required. Overshoot = illegal move.

Illegal Move Penalty

3 illegal attempts ‚Üí turn skipped (counter resets after the skip).

Bonus Roll

Capture or reaching the Core grants a bonus roll (if you implement dice later).
